Anthropic launched the Claude Partner Network in March with a $100 million commitment supporting partner education and shared marketing, attracting over 40,000 applicants with more than 10,000 consultants obtaining Claude certification. The announcement introduces two ecosystem tools: the Services Track provides a tiered framework reflecting firms' documented Claude implementations, while the Claude Partner Hub functions as a directory connecting customers with qualified service providers. Major professional services organizations including Accenture, Cognizant, Deloitte, KPMG, Infosys, and PwC are establishing Claude practices.
