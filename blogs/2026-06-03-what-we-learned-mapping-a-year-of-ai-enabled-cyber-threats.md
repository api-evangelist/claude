Anthropic's Frontier Red Team analyzed 832 accounts banned for malicious cyber activity between March 2025 and March 2026, mapping them against the MITRE ATT&CK framework and publishing results partially in Verizon's 2026 Data Breach Investigations Report. The analysis found that threat actors increasingly leverage AI during complex later stages of attacks, with cyberattacks becoming more autonomous as AI enables attackers to chain operations together. Between the two six-month periods studied, actors classified as medium-risk or higher increased from 33% to 56%, suggesting attackers are growing more sophisticated overall.
