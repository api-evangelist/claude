Anthropic has established a Seoul office and signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT to promote responsible AI adoption. The announcement highlights Claude adoption across major Korean enterprises including NAVER, Samsung SDS, LG CNS, and Nexon, alongside collaborations with academic institutions and nonprofits on AI safety research.
